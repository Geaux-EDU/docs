For deployments that do not fall into the three application types (i.e. web service, worker, and cron job), you can deploy them as add-ons on Porter. Below is the list of add-ons that are currently supported.

* [PostgreSQL](/deploying-addons/postgresql)
* [Redis](/deploying-addons/redis)
* [MongoDB](/deploying-addons/mongodb)
* [Datadog](/deploying-addons/datadog)
* [Tailscale](/deploying-addons/tailscale)
* [Wallarm](/deploying-addons/wallarm)
* [Strapi](/deploying-addons/strapi)
